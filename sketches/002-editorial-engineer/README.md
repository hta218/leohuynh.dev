## Variant: Editorial Engineer

### Design stance
Make the site feel like a calm engineering publication: readable, search-friendly, and timeless.

### Key choices
- Layout: large editorial hero, featured article, notebook rows.
- Typography: serif headlines with clean sans body.
- Color: warm paper background, ink text, terracotta accent.
- Interaction: airy/compact view toggle.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: reading, SEO, credibility, long-form writing.
- Weak at: less distinctive as a personal operating dashboard.

### Best for
A blog-first v4 where organic search and article reading matter most.
