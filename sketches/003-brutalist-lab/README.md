## Variant: Brutalist Notebook / Lab

### Design stance
Give leohuynh.dev a loud, memorable identity: an engineering lab with raw borders, terminal energy, and strong categorization.

### Key choices
- Layout: hard grid, terminal hero, filterable content cards.
- Typography: heavy sans + monospace labels.
- Color: acid yellow, black, blue, pink, green.
- Interaction: content kind filters.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: unique personality, memorability, developer taste.
- Weak at: polarizing; article pages must be calmer to avoid fatigue.

### Best for
A v4 that intentionally avoids generic SaaS polish and wants to feel more human/experimental.
